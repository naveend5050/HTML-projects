Gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • gulab jamun mix powder
  • sugar
  • oil
  • ilache powder

Instructions

  1. step 1:to mix powder using some amount of watter and keep aside min half an hour.
  2. Step 2:after it can be divided into a small small parts and prepare round shape balls.
  3. Step 3:make the sugar sirup add ilachi powder and other side take a pan boil oil at low flame.
  4. Step 4:one by one dip into oil after deepfry take out and dipped into the sugar sirup.
